# TaskFlow Business Model 💰

## Revenue Streams

### 1. Subscription Plans

#### Free Plan
- **Price**: $0/month
- **Target**: Individual users, small teams testing the product
- **Features**:
  - Up to 5 team members
  - Unlimited tasks
  - Basic task management
  - Email support
- **Goal**: Customer acquisition, build user base

#### Pro Plan
- **Price**: $9/month (annual discount available)
- **Target**: Small to medium teams (5-50 people)
- **Features**:
  - Up to 50 team members
  - Unlimited tasks
  - Advanced analytics
  - Priority email support
  - API access
  - Custom integrations
- **Goal**: Main revenue driver, customer retention

#### Enterprise Plan
- **Price**: $49/month (minimum 100 seats)
- **Target**: Large organizations
- **Features**:
  - Unlimited team members
  - Unlimited tasks
  - Advanced security & SSO
  - Dedicated account manager
  - Custom integrations
  - Phone support 24/7
  - SLA guarantee
  - On-premise deployment option
- **Goal**: Premium revenue, B2B partnerships

### 2. Add-on Services

#### Premium Support Package
- **Price**: $29/month
- **Includes**: Priority support, custom training, onboarding

#### Integration Marketplace
- **Commission**: 20% on third-party integrations
- **Target**: Slack, Microsoft Teams, Jira, Monday.com connectors

#### Professional Services
- **Implementation**: $2,000 - $10,000
- **Training**: $500/day
- **Custom development**: $150/hour

## Pricing Strategy

### Unit Economics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) | $50 |
| Lifetime Value (LTV) | $1,200 |
| LTV:CAC Ratio | 24:1 ✓ |
| Payback Period | 6 months |

### Pricing Tiers Justification

- **Free**: Converts 3-5% to paid (viral growth)
- **Pro**: Targets 60% of revenue (volume)
- **Enterprise**: Targets 35% of revenue (high value)

## Customer Acquisition Strategy

### 1. Organic Growth (40%)
- SEO optimization for task management keywords
- Content marketing (blog, tutorials, webinars)

### 2. Paid Advertising (35%)
- Google Ads ($2,000/month budget)
- LinkedIn Ads for B2B ($1,500/month)
- Product Hunt launch ($5,000 campaign)

### 3. Partnerships (15%)
- Integrations with popular tools
- Affiliate partnerships
- Agency partnerships

### 4. Community Building (10%)
- GitHub open-source initiatives
- Discord community
- User group events

## Risk Mitigation

### Market Risk
- **Risk**: Low market demand
- **Mitigation**: Early customer interviews, MVP validation, feedback loops

### Competition Risk
- **Risk**: Competitors enter market
- **Mitigation**: Focus on UX, build network effects, customer lock-in

### Technical Risk
- **Risk**: Scalability issues
- **Mitigation**: Architect for scale, use managed services, load testing

### Churn Risk
- **Risk**: High customer churn
- **Mitigation**: Focus on retention, customer success programs, NPS tracking
